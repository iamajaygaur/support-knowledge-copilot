# Billing FAQ

## When are invoices generated?

Invoices are generated on the **1st of each month** for the previous billing period. Annual plans are invoiced on the subscription anniversary date.

## Payment methods

We accept major credit cards and ACH for invoices over **$1,000**. Wire transfers require approval from Finance.

## Failed payments

After a failed payment, the account enters a **7-day grace period**. On day 8, paid features are limited until payment succeeds. Error code **BILL-4020** indicates a declined card.
