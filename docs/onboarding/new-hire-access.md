# New Hire Access Checklist

## Day 0

HR creates the employee profile in Workday. An account provisioning job runs every **15 minutes** and creates the product user with role `member`.

## Day 1

The hiring manager requests additional roles in **Access Requests**. Admin roles require approval from IT Security and are granted within **1 business day**.

## Tools included by default

- Product workspace
- Company email
- Slack workspace
- Notion employee handbook

VPN access is **not** granted by default and must be requested separately with business justification.
