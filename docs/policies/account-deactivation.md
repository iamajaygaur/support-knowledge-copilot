# Account Deactivation Policy

## Employee offboarding

When HR marks an employee as terminated in Workday, product access is revoked within **1 hour**. Shared credentials must be rotated the same day.

## Customer account suspension

Accounts may be suspended for non-payment after the **7-day grace period**, or for policy violations after Security review.

## Data access after deactivation

Deactivated users cannot log in. Managers may request a **30-day** data handoff package through Support.
