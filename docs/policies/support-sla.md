# Support SLA

## Response times

| Priority | First response | Resolution target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Urgent | 15 minutes | 4 hours |
| High | 1 hour | 1 business day |
| Medium | 4 hours | 3 business days |
| Low | 1 business day | 5 business days |

## Business hours

Support business hours are **Monday–Friday, 08:00–18:00 local site time**, excluding company holidays.
